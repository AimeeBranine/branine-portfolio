Frame-by-Frame 2024

It is 1933 and you are a silent film actress about to star in her first 'talkie' film. However, when someone on the film is murdered a mysterious entity reveals itself to you. It agrees to help you find the murderer but at a great cost should you get it wrong. Use public domain films try to find out who among the studio is the murderer.


This was a solo-dev game done for my senior Capstone project at UCCS and was inspired by old Hollywood films and film history.
