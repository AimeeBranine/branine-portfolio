Penguin Noir 2022-2023

Penguin Noir is a comedic pinball-inspired platformer where the player is Giuseppe Squawks a private eye who, along with his friend Tony the Octopus, is trying to solve the mystery of his girlfriend's disappearance.

For this game, I worked on initial character concepts, background art, UI, tilesets and comic book pages.
