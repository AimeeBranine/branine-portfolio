  • Introduction to the Bachelor of Innovation
  • The Bachelor of Innovation (BI) is a fully accredited degree program, the only one of its kind in the country.
    The BI encompasses all colleges with twenty-two majors falling under the Bachelor of Innovation. The BI has a focus on cross-functional, multi-disciplinary coursework that encourages collaboration between students from various areas of study.

    Through this program, students not only learn about entrepreneurship and innovation but also practice it through ontological learning, or learning by doing.

    An example of this style of learning is the three-part Innovation Teams courses. In these classes, students are placed on a student-led team and assigned a client. Throughout the semester, the team of students work together and alongside the client to meet the client's goals, culminating in a final report that is given to the professors.
